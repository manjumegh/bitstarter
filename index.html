About

"Do Charity, and Count Your Blessings"
Charity-Box is social networking site with the cause. This is little effort to reduce the gap between poor/needy and rich people. By providing financial/social help on right time (when there is need) and from right people (connected, affordable people) to right people (authenticated individual). The needy user or their friends or relatives can post need request through Charity-Box . The request will be broadcasted, with posting user's relation. The Charity-Box through social networking will provide help by reaching to right persons. In return of charity, the donors will recieves Charity-Points, equal to donation/charity made by an individual. The value of participants are their Charity-Points. Charity-Points are measure of an individuals' service/donation. With every intellectual/material donation/charity, the reciever of charity will give charity points to donar. More the charity point more and on time help will be given when an individual will be in a need. The Charity-Pointsare transferrable from one individaul to other individual in network.

How does it work/function?

  • Creating individual's Profile page.
    • Enter your professional information - Required for Intellectual Charity.
    • Enter your personal information - Required for Materialistic Charity.
  • Adding friends and relatives for growing network.
  • Sample profile page and post is shown by clicking on link.
