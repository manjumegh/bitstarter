About

"Do Charity, and Count Your Blessings"
Charity-Box is social networking site with the cause. This is little effort to reduce the gap between poor/needy and rich people. By providing financial/social help on right time (when there is need) and from right people (connected, affordable people) to right people (authenticated individual). The needy user (poors or victims of riots/attacks or natural calamities (earthquake/flood/tsunami)) or their friends or relatives will post need request through Charity-Box. The request will be broadcasted, with posting user's relation. The Charity-Box through social networking will provide help by reaching to right persons. In return of charity, the donors will recieves Charity-Points, equal to donation/charity made by an individual. The value of participants are their Charity-Points. Charity-Points are measure of an individuals' service/donation. With every intellectual/material donation/charity, the reciever of charity will give charity points to donar. More the charity point more and on time help will be given when an individual will be in a need. The Charity-Pointsare transferrable from one individaul to other individual in network.

How does it work/function?

  • Creating individual's Profile page.
    • Enter your professional information - Required for Intellectual Charity.
    • Enter your personal information - Required for Materialistic Charity.
  • Adding friends and relatives for growing network.
  • Sample profile page and post is shown by clicking on link.

FAQ

How is Charity-Box different from other Social networking sites ?
In Charity-Box is social networking site with the cause. Its an effort to reduce the increasing gap between rich and poor people. It achieves this by providing the help at right time (using networking) to right people (authenticated needy people social network) from right people (an affordable known people). This way the people in need whether an individual himself or individual's friend/relative, get authentication of "legal" inherently by using social-networking strength. It works dynamically/interactively. As shown in Fig. :, e.g., An individual's friend needs $10,000 for his father's knee surgery(met with an accident) and blood (group O+). He will raise request on Charity-Box, as shown. There are 3 tabs etc etc.
How does it make money?
The Charity-Box is social networking site, with following entities.
  • Commodities: These are the consumables in the social networking. They are as follows:
    • Intellectual Charity: These commodities includes teaching to poor/blind/underpreivilage people. Mentoring the students/graduates in studies or choosing carrier. Donating time for old-age homes and orphanages. For users below 15years, they can collect charity points by growing plants/trees and taking care or environment. They can save/accumulates Charity-Points for their future, e.g., higher education.
    • Material Charity: It includes money for medical purpose, for victims of riots/natural disasters. Blood and organ donation. For users below 15years, by donating used clothes, toys or other accesseries, e.g., pens/pencils/notebooks etc etc.
  • Consumers: They are the one who raise the request. They can be an individual or organization (NGOs e.g. orphanages), directly/indirectly consuming commodities.
  • Suppliers: They are the one who can afford to provide help to needy (consumers). They can be an individual or organization (NGOs e.g. orphanages), directly/indirectly consuming commodities.
More the intercation between entities, more the users and more the advertisements from other commodities.
  • It makes money, from advertisement companies, by providing them user's interests.
  • Get profit by providing charity to hospitals, other NGOs connected with the site.
  • It makes money from universities/colleges or startups/companies, who wants to seek consultancy or working hours of suppliers.
